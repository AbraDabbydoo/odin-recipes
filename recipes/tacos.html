John's Tasty Tacos

The Best Tacos on Earth

Only using the freshest ingredients, you can have the freshest tacos. Follow along on your taco Tuesday!

Ingredients

  • Taco Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Cilantro
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef. Add taco seasoning while cooking. Drain the fat.
  2. Put shells on plate and meat in shells
  3. Add cheese, lettuce, cilanto,tomato and salsa on top of beef

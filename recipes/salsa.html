John's Wild Salsa

The World's Best Salsa

Only using the freshest ingredients, you can have the freshest salsa. Follow along on your taco Tuesday!

Ingredients

  • 1 Jalapano
  • 4 Tomatos
  • 1 Habanaro
  • 1 Handful Cilantro

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to blender and pulse till salsa is combined.
